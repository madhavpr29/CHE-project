About Us

Our mission is to empower individuals, communities, and organizations with the knowledge and resources needed to address the critical challenges surrounding water availability.

WHAT WE DO

One of the primary functions of the Aqua harmony is to fund research related to water. The organization provides grants to researchers and institutions around the world to support projects that advance our understanding of water quality, treatment, and distribution.
